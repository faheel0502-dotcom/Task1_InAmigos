Who We Are

Spreading Joy and Building a Sustainable Future

Established on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization focused on uplifting underprivileged lives. Our operations are fully transparent and certified under 80G, 12A, CSR-1, and ISO 9001:2015 standards.

Through our dedicated network of interns, volunteers, and partners, we drive impactful campaigns in slums, rural regions, and urban centers to tackle education gaps, hunger, environmental neglect, and animal welfare issues.

Our Core Projects

We operate multiple tailored programs addressing specific challenges faced by local communities, animals, and the ecosystem.

Project Bachpanshala

Education & Mentorship

Providing high-quality informal education, basic supplies, and interactive learning environments to kids in slum areas to prepare them for a brighter future.

Project Seva

Hunger Alleviation

Striving for zero hunger by organizing regular food distribution drives, establishing community kitchens, and supplying clothes to underprivileged people.

Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Equipping women in rural and suburban communities with vocational training, sewing skills, leadership training, and financial literacy to enable self-reliance.

Project Prakriti

Environment Sustainability

Fostering environmental preservation through tree plantation drives, clean-up operations in parks, and raising awareness about plastic reduction.

Project Jeev

Animal Welfare

Rescuing injured stray animals, conducting regular street animal feeding drives, and organizing vaccination campaigns to ensure harmony and animal safety.

Project Vikas

Skill Development

Enhancing the employability of youth through computer classes, public speaking mentoring, and workshops that prepare them for the modern job market.
